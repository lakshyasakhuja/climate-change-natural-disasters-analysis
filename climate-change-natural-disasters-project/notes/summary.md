# Summary: The Influence of Climate Change on the Frequency and Severity of Natural Disasters

This project, completed as part of DATA1001 at the University of Sydney, explores how climate change is affecting the frequency and intensity of natural disasters such as floods, cyclones, droughts, and wildfires.

It synthesises scientific literature, reports, and climate data to highlight trends linked to rising greenhouse gas emissions and global temperatures, and discusses implications for societies, economies, and the environment.
